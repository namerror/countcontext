ChatGPT Context Counter

How We Estimate Context Usage

This extension estimates how much of your current ChatGPT conversation context is in use. It is helpful for planning prompts, but it should be treated as an estimate rather than an exact internal ChatGPT meter.

What Works Today

  • We read and filter conversation text from the current ChatGPT page.
  • We estimate chat tokens and add a fixed overhead reserve.
  • We show total estimated tokens, percent used, and context size.
  • We offer two methods: Fast (heuristic) and Precise (tokenizer-based).
  • We warn when older history may not be fully loaded in the page.
  • We remember your selected plan, model, and estimation method locally in your browser.

What Does Not Work Yet

  • Attachments are not implemented and are not counted.
  • Automatic model detection is not implemented yet.
  • ChatGPT Projects or project-wide context are not supported.
  • Some conversation history may not be counted until ChatGPT loads it into the page.

How To Use The Estimate

  • Open the panel from the bottom-right corner of ChatGPT.
  • Select your plan and model manually.
  • Pick Fast for speed or Precise for a closer estimate.
  • Click Recalculate after scrolling up or loading more history.
  • If the extension warns that history may be incomplete, treat the total as low.
  • On very large chats, Precise may fall back to Fast automatically.

What The Numbers Mean

  • Chat text: estimated from conversation text in the page.
  • Overhead: fixed 500-token reserve for system or formatting costs.
  • Attachments: shown as not implemented and excluded from totals.
  • Fast: simple character-based estimate.
  • Precise: bundled tokenizer estimate.

The estimate is informational only. A fully loaded conversation and the right manual plan and model selection will usually give the best result.

Privacy Summary

  • The extension reads the current ChatGPT page so it can estimate token usage.
  • Processing happens locally in your browser.
  • Your settings are stored locally with Chrome extension storage.
  • The extension does not send your conversation contents to an external server.
